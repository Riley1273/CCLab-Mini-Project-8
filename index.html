The Four Mirrors

Project Description

This project allows you to sketch, not on a normal canvas, but rather on one that is composed of 4 mirros. Anything drawn on the canvas will be reflected into the 3 other quadrants of the canvas as well, allowing for perfectly symetric and geometic drawings.

Instructions

1) Hold down your mouse and drag over the canvas to paint.
2) To change pen color, press SHIFT.
3) To change pen size, press the left arrow key.
4) To restart, refresh the page.
